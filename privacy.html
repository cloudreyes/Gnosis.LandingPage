1. INTRODUCTION

Gnosis AI ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Apple Watch application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Voice Input and Conversations

  • When you speak to Gnosis AI, your voice is converted to text on your device
  • Your questions and the AI responses are temporarily processed to provide the service
  • Conversation history is stored locally on your Apple Watch and paired iPhone

Device Information

  • Basic device information required for app functionality
  • No personal identifiers are collected

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide AI-powered responses to your questions
  • To maintain conversation history for contextual dialogue
  • To enable the core functionality of the app

Important: Your conversations are sent to these AI services in real-time to generate responses. This data transmission is necessary for the app to function.

5. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION

Local Storage Only

  • All conversation history is stored locally on your Apple Watch and paired iPhone
  • We do NOT store your conversations on our servers
  • Data is stored using standard Apple frameworks (Core Data, UserDefaults, iCloud sync if enabled)
  • You can delete conversation history at any time within the app
  • All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

6. DATA SHARING

  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do NOT use advertising or tracking technologies
  • Data is only shared with AI service providers as necessary to generate responses
  • We do receive aggregate statistics or analytics from Apple App Store Connect (downloads, crashes, basic usage)

7. ANALYTICS AND TRACKING

  • We do NOT use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT track your behavior or usage patterns
  • Basic app statistics (downloads, active devices, crashes) are provided by Apple and do not identify individual users
  • No cookies or tracking pixels are used

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Gnosis AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Delete all conversation history within the app settings
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Remove all locally stored data by deleting the app
  • Contact us with privacy questions or concerns

10. DATA SECURITY

  • Conversations are transmitted securely (HTTPS/TLS) to AI service providers
  • Local data is protected by your device's security features
  • We implement industry-standard security practices

11. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

AI service providers may process data in countries outside your residence. By using Gnosis AI, you consent to this transfer and processing.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy at gnosisai.app/privacy
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • (Optional) In-app notification for material changes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

California residents: We do not sell personal information. You have rights under CCPA to request information about data collection and deletion.
